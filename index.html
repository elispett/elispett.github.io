About me

Hi! I'm a PhD student in Computational Biology at the University of Trento and COSBI. I'm curious by nature and passionate about learning — especially when it comes to understanding how biology works through the lens of math and code.

My research focuses on physiologically based pharmacokinetic (PBPK) modeling, particularly of RNA-based therapies. I develop models to describe how RNA is trafficked in the body and how it gets metabolized to produce cutting-edge therapies.
